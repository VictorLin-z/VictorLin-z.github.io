Intelligent Decision Analysis of Converter Station SER Based on FP-Growth Algorithm

(IEEE Sustainable Power and Energy Conference, iSPEC2021)

Abstract

In order to improve the efficiency of operation and maintenance personnel in the research and judgment of the massive SER data generated by the converter station protection and enhance the converter station protection capability, a method based on FP-Growth algorithm for intelligent Decision Analysis of Converter Station SER. A multi-dimensional model is established for SER when converter station fails using data cleaning and association rule mining methods, and FP-Growth algorithm is used for data mining and analysis to obtain SER fault credible group. Then use SER fault credible group to determine the probability of SER abnormality. It facilitates the operation and maintenance personnel of the converter station to discover the abnormal operation of the converter station equipment in time, effectively improves the reliability of the converter station, and reduces the possibility of missed and misread events caused by manual interrogation of SER. Through analysis of the real SER data of the Kun-Bei converter station, it shows that the proposed method can effectively mine SER anomalies and improve the reliability of the converter station.
