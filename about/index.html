Project

1.Research and development of intelligent auxiliary decision-making and execution platform based on big data of DC control and protection system in Kunbei converter station (China Southern Power Grid)

  Main Contribution: Collate and analyze the trial operation data of Kunbei converter station, and carry out the association rule modeling of converter station events, realize the association rule mining between converter station actions and events, in order to meet the missing situation of generated events that can be found by operation and maintenance personnel in time when Kunliulong DC converter station state transitions or faults, the related research results are published in Power System Automation, power system protection and control, etc., and applied for one invention patent.

2.Research and application of adjustable capacity correction technology for offshore wind power grid connection based on real-time calculation of submarine cable line loss (Jiangsu Fangtian Electric Power)

  Main Contribution: For the problem of poor power foresight of offshore wind power grid-connected networks leading to the lack of adjustable capacity, a research of offshore wind farm power prediction based on deep learning algorithm is proposed, and ultra-short-term power prediction considering the spatial and temporal characteristics of offshore wind power multi-units, and the related research results are in power system automation. Boltzmann fitting with PSO-LSTM algorithm is used to forecast offshore wind farms, and the related research results are published in ISPEC2021.

3.Research on network-load coordination control auxiliary service technology for large users under asynchronous grid (Yunnan Grid)

  Main Contribution: Considering that large industrial users represented by electrolytic aluminum have flexible frequency regulation capability, study the cooperative game model for electrolytic aluminum users to participate in the auxiliary frequency regulation service market, and introduce the multi-objective optimization algorithm MOEA/D to solve the game model.

4.Research on the mechanism of multi-source cooperative operation adapted to the construction of new power system (NSFC, submitted)

  Main Contribution: To propose the research content and scientific problems, and use them to design the corresponding technical routes, and complete the project outline writing and refinement work.

5.Research and application of planning and operation of energy-efficient active intelligent flexible charging system applicable to multiple scenarios (Yunnan Power Grid)
