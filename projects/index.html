  • Research and application of adjustable capacity correction technology for offshore wind power grid-connected power based on real-time calculation of submarine cable loss (基于海缆线损实时计算的海上风电并网功率可调容量修正技术研究与应用)

    Source: Jiangsu Fangtian Electric Power Technology Co., Ltd. (江苏方天电力技术有限责任公司)

    Work: Aiming at the problem of lack of adjustable capacity due to poor power predictability of offshore wind power grid-connected points, a research on offshore wind farm power prediction based on deep learning algorithm is proposed, and ultra-short-term power prediction is carried out considering the spatiotemporal and temporal characteristics of offshore wind power multi-units. The relevant research results are in power system automation. The Boltzmann curve model is used to fit wind speed-power and the LSTM algorithm is used to predict the single unit of offshore wind power, and the Transformer model is used to predict ultra-short-term wind power. The relevant research results were published in Power System Automation and IEEE conferences.

  • Research on auxiliary service technology of network-load coordinated control for large users under asynchronous power grid (异步电网下大用户的网-荷协调控制辅助服务技术研究)

    Source: Yunnan Power Grid Co., Ltd. (云南电网有限责任公司)

    Work: Considering that large industrial users represented by electrolytic aluminum have flexible frequency regulation capabilities, on the basis and framework of previous laboratory work, the cooperative game model of electrolytic aluminum customer engagement auxiliary frequency regulation service market under the timing of power grid frequency regulation construction is studied., which is manifested as electrolytic aluminum users, conventional frequency modulation units, and power grids in the frequency regulation market to achieve price determination and frequency modulation mileage clearing under price guidance and information interaction, and introduce multi-objective optimization algorithm MOEA/D to solve the game model Pareto solution set and realize Nash equilibrium among multiple agents.
