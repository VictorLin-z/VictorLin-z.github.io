About me

I am currently pursuing a doctoral degree in electrical engineering at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology.At the present research stage, I think there is a lot of crossover between systematics and operations research, artificial intelligence and other disciplines, and the power system, as a branch of systematics, is essentially a huge information-physical system, so how to effectively utilize data-driven methods and linear planning, nonlinear programming, game theory in operations research to achieve information analysis and computation, and artificial intelligence methods (including deep learning and reinforcement learning under machine learning) to achieve the simulation of physical equipment to achieve the effective coupling of the information system of the power system and the physical system, is an important direction for me to break through in the future study.
